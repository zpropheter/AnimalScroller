AnimalScroller

Is your kid interested in animals? Want to show them a random collection of animals with labels on each? Try this out. It's an XCode project to automatically scroll photos of animals loaded from wikipedia.
Designed purely as a PoC. 

If you were to pursue this further, there's better validation that could be done in the Wikipedia Service as it will accidentally catch animal related articles but not actually show the animal, just a random picture.

Also, the default 40 photos still repeatedly load even after it's fetched the wikipedia stream.
